### Threats
- BiggerPockets combines the highest domain authority in the niche with dedicated per-keyword calculator landing pages and a large blog covering every FlipIQ keyword (fix & flip, BRRRR, ARV, 70% rule, rental cash flow). It is the hardest organic competitor to outrank.
- REIkit runs FREE, ungated standalone calculators for ARV and the 70% Rule at tools.reikit.com (no signup required). Free tools tend to outrank signup-gated tools — direct pressure on FlipIQ's arv-calculator and 70-rule-calculator pages.
- DealCheck pairs a strong, low-price freemium model ($0 / $10 / $20) with dedicated SEO feature pages for house flipping, BRRRR, rental, multi-family and wholesaling calculators — a strong price + SEO combination overlapping most of FlipIQ's lineup.
- FlipperForce ships product and content rapidly (AI Receipt Analyzer, monthly feature blog posts) keeping pages fresh, which aids SEO recency signals.

### Opportunities
- FlipIQ already publishes dedicated hard-money-loan, house-flipping-cost-estimator and house-flipping-profit calculators that competitors either bundle into a broader flip tool or omit entirely. These are lower-competition keywords FlipIQ is well-positioned to OWN — prioritize SEO/content/backlinks to rank them.
- Keep FlipIQ's calculators ungated (like REIkit's free ARV/70% tools) and convert via the deal-checklist PDF lead magnet, rather than gating behind signup — supports the 5% email-capture goal.
- Pricing/simplicity wedge: DealCheck ($0–$20) and BiggerPockets (~$390/yr) anchor low while FlipperForce ($49–$499/mo) and REIkit ($64–$204/mo) are expensive/feature-heavy. A focused, affordable flip-calculator product has positioning room.
- Tooling FlipIQ lacks that competitors have (rehab cost estimator, comps/ARV data backing, multi-family/STR calculators) — optional future roadmap; adds data cost/complexity, so lower priority than owning flip keywords.

### Validates
- All four competitors offer Fix & Flip + BRRRR + ARV + 70% Rule tooling — confirms FlipIQ's core calculator lineup matches the highest-demand keywords (supports the organic-traffic goal).
- Freemium + free trial with no credit card is universal across all four — validates FlipIQ's freemium / email-capture funnel.
- Dedicated per-keyword SEO landing pages are standard practice (DealCheck, BiggerPockets, FlipperForce) — validates FlipIQ's one-page-per-calculator structure.
- Free standalone calculators as lead magnets (REIkit) validate FlipIQ's free-tool + deal-checklist-PDF approach to driving email capture.
