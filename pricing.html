What each feature actually does

Not just a feature list — here's how each one saves you time and money.

Lender-Ready PDF Report

One click generates a clean, professional PDF summarizing your deal assumptions, cost breakdown, projected profit, and ROI. Formatted to hand to a hard money lender or private investor without any extra work on your end. No more emailing spreadsheets or explaining numbers on the phone.

Pro

Sensitivity Analysis

See exactly how your profit changes if the ARV comes in lower than expected, or if rehab costs run over. FlipIQ generates a grid showing profit across different ARV and rehab scenarios so you know your downside before you make an offer — not after you're already under contract.

Pro

BRRRR Analysis

Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat — the BRRRR strategy has its own dedicated analysis tab. Enter your rental income, refi terms, and holding costs and FlipIQ calculates whether you can pull your capital back out, what your cash-on-cash return looks like, and whether the deal works long-term as a rental.

Pro

ARV Comp Calculator

After-repair value is the most important number in a flip — and the hardest to nail down. The ARV comp tool lets you enter up to five comparable sales with square footage, condition, and price, and automatically weights them to produce a defensible ARV estimate you can show lenders or use to double-check your agent's number.

Pro

Deal Pipeline

Track every deal you're evaluating in one place. Deals move through stages — Prospecting, Under Contract, Rehab, Listed, Sold — so you can see your whole pipeline at a glance. Never lose track of a deal you analyzed three weeks ago, or forget where you left off on a property.

Pro

Flip Timeline Tracker

Set your expected close, rehab start, rehab complete, and list dates — and FlipIQ tracks your carrying cost burn automatically as time passes. This keeps your profit projection honest as the project drags on, and gives you a clear picture of how holding cost erosion affects your bottom line week by week.

Pro

Send to Lender

Package your deal analysis into a clean, email-formatted summary and send it directly to your hard money lender or private money contact from inside FlipIQ. Includes the deal address, key numbers, exit strategy, and your PDF attachment — everything a lender needs to pre-approve you quickly.

Pro

Deal Memo PDF

The deal memo is a more detailed version of the lender report — it includes your investment thesis, comparable sales, rehab scope summary, risk notes, and projected timeline. Use it when you want to present a deal to a business partner, private investor, or anyone who needs more context than raw numbers.

Pro

Deal Sharing

Generate a read-only shareable link to any saved deal. Your partner, contractor, or agent can pull it up on their phone without needing a FlipIQ account. No more screenshotting spreadsheets or trying to describe numbers over the phone.

Pro

CSV Export

Export your full deal analysis — every input and output — as a CSV file. Drop it into Excel or Google Sheets for custom modeling, hand it to your accountant, or build your own tracking system on top of FlipIQ's calculations. Your data stays yours.

Pro

Carrying Costs & Loan Interest

Free-tier analyses estimate the basics. Pro adds the full cost picture: monthly carrying costs (utilities, insurance, taxes, HOA), hard money loan origination fees and interest payments calculated across your hold period, and a day-by-day interest accrual breakdown. This is what separates a back-of-napkin estimate from an accurate profit projection.

Pro

Deal Score (Free)

Every analysis — even on the free tier — gets a letter grade from A to F based on your profit margin, ROI, and deal risk factors. It's a fast gut-check that cuts through the numbers: a B+ is a solid deal worth pursuing, a D means walk away unless you can renegotiate the price. Comes with a plain-English narrative explaining the score.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the free tier include?
The free tier gives you 5 deal analyses with profit, ROI, max allowable offer, deal score, and a basic cost breakdown. It's enough to evaluate a deal and see if it's worth digging deeper — but it doesn't include carrying costs, PDF reports, or deal saving.
Can I cancel Pro anytime?
Yes. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Monthly subscribers can cancel before their next billing date. Annual subscribers can cancel at any time and keep access through the end of the paid year.
Does Pro cover multiple properties at once?
Yes — Pro gives you unlimited analyses and deal saving, so you can run as many properties as you want simultaneously, track each one through your pipeline, and compare them side by side.
Is there a free trial of Pro?
There's no time-limited trial, but the free tier lets you run 5 full analyses (including deal score) before committing. Most investors know whether FlipIQ is a fit within the first deal or two.
What payment methods does FlipIQ accept?
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through Stripe. Payments are processed securely — FlipIQ never stores your card details.
Does FlipIQ work on mobile?
Yes. FlipIQ is fully responsive and installable as a PWA (progressive web app) on iOS and Android. Tap "Add to Home Screen" in your browser and it runs like a native app — no App Store required.
Can I use FlipIQ for rental properties, not just flips?
Yes. Pro includes a BRRRR analysis tab and the deal analyzer covers rental cash flow scenarios alongside the flip exit strategy. FlipIQ is built for investors who run multiple strategies — flip, hold, or BRRRR — and want to compare them side by side for the same property.
